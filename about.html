about me

Hello! My name is Ashley Bates and I am a Junior at Indiana University Bloomington studying Informatics with a cognate in Graphic Design and a minor in Human-Centered Computing.
I come from a fine arts background but decided to switch to STEM. I wanted to dive into something different than what I was learning before. I wanted to bring my designs to life; I wanted to make them dynamic. I am now fascinated with coding and the logic behind writing algorithms. I am driven to achieve. Design is a process of iteration and I can always improve what I make today.
